About R2
Established in 1991, R2 Enterprises Ltd is an Auckland based importer and wholesaler of motorcycle & ATV parts and accessories that deals with all major bike dealers, mechanics, and bike shops up and down New Zealand, offering them great prices and quality, without skimping on service.

We source parts specific for New Zealand conditions, and through extensive research and development, offer our dealers comprehensive fitments listings so they know exactly what parts they need to service, repair, or upgrade their vehicle. With parts sourced from as far away as Scandinavia, the team here at R2 ensure that no rocks are left unturned, and no catalogue left unread to bring you the most up to date Sprint3 fitments within the country.

Through years of being a major player in the motorcycle parts industry, R2 has even developed a specialty line of products under the banner of our very own brand, Kiwi-X.

Kiwi-X offers the customer a range of parts that are researched, developed, tested, and manufactured specifically for New Zealand conditions.
